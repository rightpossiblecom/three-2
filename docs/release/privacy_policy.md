# Privacy Policy for Three.

**Last updated: February 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Three. mobile application (the "App").

## Data Collection and Use
**We do not collect any personal data**. Three. is designed with privacy as the core fundamental:
- **No User Accounts:** There is no registration or signup required to use the App.
- **Local Storage:** All your data, tasks, and history are stored locally on your device using encrypted/secure local storage methods. Nothing is sent to external servers or cloud services.
- **No Tracking or Analytics:** We do not track your usage behavior, nor do we employ third-party analytics (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel).
- **No Personal Information:** We do not request names, emails, contacts, locations, or financial data.

## Play Store Data Safety 
When interacting with the Google Play Store Data Safety form, we declare that:
- No data is collected.
- No data is shared with third parties.
- All App data is stored strictly locally.

## Contact Us
If you have questions about our privacy practices, you may contact us at: support@three.app
